Refund Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

This Refund Policy describes refund eligibility and procedures for purchases made directly through Creatorrr for Contentorrr and related Services.

1. General policy

We want you to have confidence in your purchase. If you are not satisfied, contact us and we will review your request in good faith under the criteria below.

2. Eligibility windows

  • New subscriptions: refund requests submitted within 14 calendar days of first purchase may be eligible.
  • Renewals: refund requests submitted within 7 calendar days of renewal may be eligible.
  • One-time purchases: refund requests submitted within 14 calendar days of purchase may be eligible.

3. What may reduce or prevent refund eligibility

  • Evidence of extensive usage inconsistent with trial/evaluation intent.
  • Repeated refund requests indicating abusive or fraudulent behavior.
  • Violations of our Terms of Use.
  • Purchases made through third-party platforms with separate billing policies.

4. Non-refundable items

  • Taxes, duties, or fees paid to third parties/governments (unless required by law).
  • Custom services, consulting, implementation, or enterprise onboarding fees (unless agreed otherwise in writing).
  • Charges outside the eligibility window, subject to mandatory local law.

5. How to request a refund

  1. Email ben@creatorrr.com from the billing account email.
  2. Include purchase date, invoice/receipt details, and the reason for your request.
  3. Allow up to 5 business days for initial review and response.

6. Processing timeline

If approved, refunds are typically issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Your bank or card issuer may require additional posting time.

7. Chargebacks

Before initiating a chargeback, please contact us so we can try to resolve billing issues quickly. Fraudulent or bad-faith chargebacks may result in account suspension.

8. Regional consumer rights

This policy does not limit mandatory consumer rights provided by applicable law. Where local law provides additional rights, those rights will apply.

9. Policy updates

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted with a new effective date.

Need help quickly? Send your request and billing receipt to ben@creatorrr.com and include “Refund Request” in the subject line.
