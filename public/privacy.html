Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Creatorrr (“Creatorrr,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with our websites, applications, and services, including Contentorrr (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account details (such as name, email address, login credentials).
  • Billing and purchase details (handled through payment processors where applicable).
  • Communications and support requests you send to us.
  • Feedback, feature requests, and product survey responses.

1.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Device and technical information (browser type, operating system, app version, diagnostics).
  • Usage information (pages viewed, feature usage, session events, timestamps, referring pages).
  • Security information (authentication events, IP-derived geolocation at coarse level, abuse signals).
  • Cookies, local storage, and similar technologies used to maintain sessions and preferences.

1.3 Content you process through the Services

If you upload, generate, organize, or manage media/content in our Services, that data may be processed to provide core product functionality such as asset generation, metadata enrichment, and library indexing.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Authenticate users and secure accounts.
  • Process transactions and deliver paid features.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate product updates.
  • Monitor reliability, detect abuse/fraud, and enforce our Terms of Use.
  • Comply with legal obligations and lawful requests.

3. Legal bases for processing (where applicable)

Depending on your location, we process personal information based on one or more legal bases, including contractual necessity, legitimate interests, consent, and compliance with legal obligations.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the Services (hosting, analytics, support, payment processing).
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, insurance) when reasonably necessary.
  • Authorities or other parties when required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Acquirers or successors in connection with a merger, financing, or sale of assets.

5. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Services, meet legal/accounting obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary by data type and purpose.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict processing of your personal data.

  • You can request account data actions by contacting us.
  • You can unsubscribe from non-essential marketing communications at any time.
  • You may adjust browser settings to manage cookies (some features may be affected).

7. International data transfers

We may process information in countries other than your own. Where required, we use safeguards intended to protect personal information during cross-border transfers.

8. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect personal information. No security system is perfect; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or older age thresholds where required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes are made, we will update the effective date and provide additional notice where required.

11. Contact us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at ben@creatorrr.com.
